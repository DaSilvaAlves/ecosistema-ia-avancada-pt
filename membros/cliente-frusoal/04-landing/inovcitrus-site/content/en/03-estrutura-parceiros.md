# Structure and Partners

> Frusoal InovCitrus is a permanent R&D centre, promoted by Frusoal and operated in partnership with national and regional scientific institutions.

---

## Promoter

### Frusoal — Frutas Sotavento Algarve, Lda

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Type | Producer Organisation (PO), recognised under the Common Agricultural Policy |
| Position | Largest Producer Organisation for citrus fruit in Portugal <!-- F04 --> |
| Headquarters | Vila Nova de Cacela, Algarve <!-- F07 --> |
| Founded | 1981 (45 years of activity in 2026) <!-- F07 --> |
| Managing partner | Pedro Madeira <!-- F07 --> |
| Commercial brands | Gomo (conventional) · Biogomo (organic) <!-- F04, F07 --> |
| Certifications | GlobalG.A.P. · GRASP · PGI Citrinos do Algarve <!-- F07 --> |

Frusoal has a track record in scientific projects on pests — notably **Fruit Fly Protec**, ongoing since 2018, on fruit flies. <!-- F06 --> InovCitrus is the institutionalisation of this effort within a permanent dedicated structure.

---

## Scientific partner

### InnovPlantProtect (InPP)

R&D centre dedicated to plant health, with the declared mission of "developing innovative biosolutions that promote plant health, and digital solutions for more precise and efficient agricultural management." <!-- F05 verbatim, paraphrased -->

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Headquarters | Elvas, Alentejo |
| Crop areas covered | Citrus growing (via InovCitrus), tomato, rice, maize, pastures, vines |
| Institutional site | [iplantprotect.pt](https://iplantprotect.pt/) |

InPP is the formal scientific partner of the first three-year Scirtothrips project, with shared responsibilities in technical coordination, methodology and scientific analysis. <!-- F01 -->

---

## Extension and field-trial partner

### CCDR Algarve · Tavira Centre for Agricultural Experimentation

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Type | Public agricultural experimentation centre |
| Location | Tavira, Algarve |
| Role in the project | Provides trial fields and experimental infrastructure to the three-year project <!-- F01 --> |

The presence of the Centre for Agricultural Experimentation ensures scientific representativeness of public trial sites, complementing the private orchards of Frusoal and its member growers.

---

## Member growers

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Number | 65 member growers <!-- F07 --> |
| Aggregate area | 1,500 hectares <!-- F07 --> |
| Aggregate annual production | 40,000 tonnes <!-- F07 --> |
| Composition of production | 80% citrus · 20% avocado, persimmon, carob <!-- F07 --> |
| Role in InovCitrus | Provision of orchards for trials + direct beneficiaries of the research |

Member growers are simultaneously Frusoal's productive base and the first beneficiaries of InovCitrus research — the results of the three-year project will be translated into operational technical sheets for the orchards.

---

## Principle of institutional articulation

| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Scientific independence | Scientific direction of the three-year project lies with the Frusoal–InPP team, in accordance with the formal partnership protocol |
| Transparency | Project results will be published in open access on the [Scientific Repository](/en/repository) page |
| Mandatory cross-tagging | All InovCitrus scientific communication identifies the partners that contributed to each output |
| Respect for existing structures | InovCitrus complements — does not replace — the scientific work of InPP, CCDR or other regional institutions |
