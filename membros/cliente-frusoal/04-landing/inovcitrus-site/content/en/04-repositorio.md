# Scientific Repository

> Permanent dissemination structure for the research results of Frusoal InovCitrus, in open access whenever scientific-property conditions allow.

---

## Dissemination policy

| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Open access by default | Public-facing documents and technical sheets will be made available on this repository |
| Identified co-authorship | Every document identifies the responsible team and the partner institutions involved (Frusoal, InPP, CCDR Algarve) |
| Multilingual | Where applicable, documents will be made available in Portuguese, English, Spanish and French |
| Versioning | Successive documents from the same project keep earlier versions accessible |

---

## Document types planned

### Technical sheets for growers

Short outreach documents (4–8 pages), in operational language, translating scientific results into field practices applicable to orchards.

### Public outreach communications

Public documents on each project, addressed to the technical community, partner retailers, regional authorities and specialised press.

### Training materials

Support documents for training sessions for member growers, agricultural technicians and institutional partners.

### Scientific publications

Coordinated with the project's scientific partners, in journals and conferences within the sector. Cross-indexed with this repository.

## How to access a document

Documents will be published directly on this page, in PDF format, with a link in each document line. Each PDF includes:

- Identification of authors and partner institutions involved
- Date of publication
- Document version
- Abstract in four languages (PT, EN, ES, FR), where applicable
