# Frusoal InovCitrus

## A Research and Development centre dedicated to Algarve citrus growing

> InovCitrus is the R&D centre of **Frusoal — Frutas Sotavento Algarve, Lda**, the largest Producer Organisation for citrus fruit in Portugal. <!-- F04 + F07 -->

## Our mission

InovCitrus was established to: <!-- F01 paraphrase -->

- **Strengthen scientific knowledge** applied to citrus growing
- **Improve prevention and control strategies** against pests and diseases
- **Study cultivation methods and practices** that contribute to a more sustainable agriculture
- **Explore new ways of valorising** by-products of citrus production

---

## Context

Algarve citrus growing today faces converging challenges: emerging pests detected from 2022 onwards, regional water stress, growing demands on traceability and sustainability, and the need to maintain the competitiveness of Algarve citrus in European markets.

InovCitrus is Frusoal's institutional response to these challenges — a permanent applied-research centre, in partnership with scientific and agricultural extension institutions, with its headquarters under construction in Tavira.

---

## First project

A three-year research project on **Scirtothrips aurantii** — the South African citrus thrips, an EU quarantine pest detected in the Algarve from 2022 — in collaboration with InnovPlantProtect and the Tavira Centre for Agricultural Experimentation (CCDR Algarve). <!-- F01, F02, F09, F11 -->
